Fun facts about Magellanic Penguin


Magellanic Penguins are named after the explorer Ferdinand Magellan whose crew first spotted them in the year 1520.
  • Swallow a lot of ocean water
  • Too much salt for their bodies to absorb
  • They excrete the extra salt with special glands located near the eyes
